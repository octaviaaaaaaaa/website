There are three original types of Nerds Gummy Cluster:

Rainbow

This is the 3oz share pouch.

The most common type of Nerds Gummy Cluster. The nerds are an assortment of flavors together in a rainbow around the gummy center. The flavors include strawberry (pink), grape (purple), orange (orange), lemonade (yellow), and sour apple (green).

The available sizes include the 3oz theater box, the 3oz peg bag, the 3oz share pouch, the 5oz peg bag, and the 8oz stand up bag.

Very Berry

This is the 3oz share pouch.

Another very common type of Nerds Gummy Cluster. This type showcases many berry flavors, including blue raspberry, strawberry, and wildberry, along with grape flavor to equalize the berry.

The available sizes include the 3oz share pouch, and the 8oz stand up bag.

Big Chewy

This is the 4.25oz theater box.

Nerds Gummy Clusters, but double the size. Big Chewy Nerds Gummy Clusters feature orange, lemon, strawberry, and grape flavors.

The available sizes include the 4oz share pouch, the 4.25oz theater box, the 6oz peg bag, the 10oz stand up bag, and the 51oz tub.
