Special Nerds

In the 2024 football season, Nerds released two limited-time Gummy Cluster flavors. These had "rivaling" flavors, representing the countless rival teams in the season.

Grape Strawberry Blitz

This is the 3oz share pouch.

This repeating flavor honors the iconic flavor combination of the classic Nerds candies themselves that were made before the gummy clusters were created. It features a mix of grape and strawberry flavors, as the name suggests.

The available sizes include the 3oz share pouch, and the 8oz stand up bag.

Berry Punch Rush

This is the 3oz share pouch.

Starring the classic USA blue, red, and white, these flavors were made to contrast the Grape Strawberry. This was a new Nerds flavor combination, featuring raspberry, cherry, and punch.

The available sizes include the 3oz share pouch, and the 8oz stand up bag.
